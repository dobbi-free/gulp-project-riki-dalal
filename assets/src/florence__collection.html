Riki Dalal Couture is pleased to announce the debut of her “Florence Collection.” Following on from her last seasons successful range, Dalal is back with an extraordinary collection that is the perfect treat for brides wanting to feel feminine and fabulous with a touch of Dalal’s edge. Designed to cater for brides seeking superior fabrics, flattering silhouettes, and unmatchable embellishments, there is something for every bride’s personal style. In keeping with Dalal’s signature approach to bridal wear, she plays upon the use of many flowing materials, soft tulles and chiffons, and the use of a variety of neutral tones.

From conception to reality, each gown has been imagined and placed along the Italian hillsides. With the town of Florence as Dalal’s muse, the pursuit of romance was sought by placing an emphasis on the elaboration of a once effortless silhouette that has morphed into something distinctive and unforgettable. The stunning, yet simple scenery in Florence, transforms with the sparkling sunlight just as Dalal’s dresses. They begin with an impressive yet classic base, and morph into a remarkable work of art. Much of the new collection allows brides to indulge in an array of garden fantasies using countless floral appliqués and embellishments with touches of colored tones. Shimmering satin and luxurious laces are used to create texture and to add that must-have feeling of decadence and indulgence. In keeping with classic Italian style, Dalal’s gowns uphold an elegant effortlessness.

Riki says: “I wanted to make this collection as elaborate as ever so brides could continue to feel unstoppable on their wedding day.” Dalal has ensured that every woman has the chance to ooze the sultry glamour she is known for by offering a variety of silhouettes for every brides style. Her personal favourites include “Valentina,” a dusty rose mermaid silhouette with beaded bodice, and “Naomi,” a stunning strapless fitted bodice with impeccable lace detail accents and flowing train. Riki Dalal is best known for her luxury feminine designs with a modern fashion-forward twist. With use of astonishing fabrics and premium sewing and construction techniques, Riki has simply outdone herself and managed to compose a bridal collection unlike no other before.
